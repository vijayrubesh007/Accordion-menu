What is blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized and secure digital ledger technology that records and verifies transactions across a network of computers.

What is cfi ?

CFI IITM (Centre for Innovation at IIT Madras) is an innovation and incubation center fostering entrepreneurship and research collaboration within the IIT Madras ecosystem.

Advantages of blockchain?

One advantage of blockchain is its transparency and immutability, which ensures that transactions recorded on the blockchain cannot be altered or deleted, promoting trust and accountability in data management.

Uses of blockchain

Blockchain is utilized for secure and transparent financial transactions with cryptocurrencies and for enhancing supply chain management by ensuring traceability and authenticity of products.
